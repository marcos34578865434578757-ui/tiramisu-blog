## What this project means to me

This is not just a blog template.  
It is the personal content base I want to keep maintaining over time.

It is meant to hold:

- writing
- projects
- reflections and methods about building with AI

## What I have focused on recently

- an admin area for managing posts and projects directly
- dark mode and language switching so the site feels complete
- cleaner page structure and motion details so browsing feels steadier

## What I want to keep improving

Next I want to continue improving bilingual content, image management, and the editing workflow until this feels like a site I can truly rely on long term.
