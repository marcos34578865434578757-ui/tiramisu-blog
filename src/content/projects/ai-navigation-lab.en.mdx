## Why I built this project

I wanted to test a specific question: when a page carries a large number of links and categories, how do you keep it efficient without making it feel crowded?

## What I focused on

- using clearer grouping to reduce information noise
- making filters feel direct instead of explanation-heavy
- observing how well AI helps with structure breakdown and component execution

## What I have learned so far

Navigation-heavy pages do not fail because they lack content.  
They fail when they feel rich at first glance but hard to scan in practice.

The real goal is not adding more cards.  
It is helping the user find the next action faster.
